Why MotorScrap?

Even if your old car is broken, doesn't start, or hasn't passed an MOT, with MotorScrap you can get paid to scrap your car. When you scrap a car with MotorScrap, not only will you get a great price instantly, but we also make it as easy as possible. After you receive a no obligation scrap car quote, you will have the price guaranteed for 3 days to make your mind up. We also help to sort the DVLA paperwork so you're no longer responsible for the car and you avoid any fines. MotorScrap are scrap car recyclers who recycle cars to strict standards in line with the law, so you can be assured that your scrap car will be recycled in the right way.
